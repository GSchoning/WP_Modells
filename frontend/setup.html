What you're looking at

Active cells are the modelled domain — the formation extent from the properties CSV.

Outcrop / recharge cells receive recharge from above. Non-outcrop cells get zero recharge.

CHD boundary cells have heads pinned to NTOP. They sit on the deep pinch-out edge so recharge has somewhere to leave the system.

No-flow boundary cells are active edge cells that don't have CHD — typically the outcrop pinch-out edge, where the boundary is a recharge inflow rather than a regional discharge.
